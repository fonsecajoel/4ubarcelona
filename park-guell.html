Private Tour of Park Güell

Explore one of Barcelona's most iconic landmarks with a private guide. This exclusive Park Güell experience brings Gaudí's imaginative world to life through vibrant mosaics, flowing forms, and panoramic city views.

Tour Highlights

  • Dragon staircase & mosaic salamander

Itinerary

1

Monumental Entrance

Admire the dragon staircase and the iconic mosaic salamander that greet visitors with Gaudí's signature color and symbolism.

2

Hypostyle Room

Walk through the forest of Doric columns and learn how this space was originally designed as a marketplace.

3

Panoramic Terrace

Enjoy the serpentine bench and take in sweeping city views from one of Barcelona's most recognizable viewpoints.

4

Landscaped Pathways

Stroll the viaducts and winding paths integrated into nature, showcasing Gaudí's harmony between architecture and landscape.
